Art Exhibition Program: Tender Spirit 2.0

First Day - April 3, 2026

Time: 2:00 PM to 7:30 PM

Opening to the public: The exhibition will open to the public. The artists will be present to showcase their works and interact with visitors.

Throughout the Exhibition - April 4 to 16, 2026

Time: 11:30 AM to 7:30 PM (closed on Sundays)

Guided Tour: A guide will accompany you to explore the exhibition.

Best Artwork Contest: The public will choose their favorite artwork.

Last Day - April 17, 2026

Time: 7:00 PM to 12:00 AM

Result Announcement: The results of the contest will be announced.

Closing Ceremony: Closing party to celebrate the artistic event.
