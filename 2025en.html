Tender Spirits Exhibition

Introduction

The Tender Spirit exhibition showcases artworks from all over the world, blending
spirituality and sensuality. The 15 featured pieces offer a new perspective on our minds,
presenting works that are as magnificent as they are unique.

Featured Artworks:

Artwork 1

MR BYSTE - Schrödinger’s Human

This interactive artwork completely breaks the traditional untouchable art concept by introducing a new format and a human-centered design, fully inviting interaction.

Artwork 2

Javier Barriga - Reminiscence

Through this piece, the artist invites us to reflect using imagination with a simple artwork that leaves room for interpretation.

Artwork 3

Insane 51 - Acanthis

Insane 51 is a dual-perspective artwork (using 3D glasses) that reveals an alternate vision of the piece.

Some Artists:

Belin

Spanish artist known for his post-neo-cubist style, blending realism and geometry.

Javier Barriga

Painter and illustrator, combining realism and abstraction with strong visual storytelling.

Insane 51

Greek street artist specializing in 3D graffiti and optical illusions.
